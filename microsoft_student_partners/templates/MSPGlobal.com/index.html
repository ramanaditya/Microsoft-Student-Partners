About

MSPGlobal Event Hosting Plateform

MSPGlobal is the all-in-one live online events platform where attendees can learn, interact, and connect with MSPs from anywhere in the world. Our main aim to spread awareness of the latest technologies and methodologies used by Industries and to find the right talents for Industries. It's an online tech contest, therefore students can participate in the quiz from anywhere and they don't need to visit any Physical center.

This program is dedicated to events, webinars organized by MSPs to host their events, where organizers and attendees can interact with each other and learn from each others. Our Mission is to help MSPs to successfully Host their events and get the huge amount of satisfied MSPs from their events. Here MSPs can host their virtual events, webinars,competetion and many more online stuffs to learn, interact and connect with each other to make event successful.

Objectives

MSPGlobal is an initiative towards enhancing programming knowledge and skills among the students. We always believe in students' bright future, and this online tech quiz competition will help students to develop their problem-solving skills, programming knacks, and the latest industrial technology awareness. It is a perfect platform to reach out to young minds and encourage them to look beyond their theoretical/conceptual knowledge and expand their aptitude knowledge in Computer World.

Our Vision & Mission

  • The mission of organizing MSPGlobal is to spread knowledge and develop programming skills, problem-solving attitude, industrial technology awareness and to connect students with industry standards.
  • This will help students to enhance their aptitude knowledge in maths and programming areas.
  • The MSPGlobal is dedicated to serving the young minds that will help them 'to think out of the box'.
  • Our aim to connect all students on a single online platform and motivate and inspire them for their bright future with self- motivation and skill development programs.
  • Our vision behind organizing a tech quiz is to build a new world in which we can enhance the potential of the students and encourage them to participate with fun as well as a competitive element in technical World.
